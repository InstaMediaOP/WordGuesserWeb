Privacy Policy + EULA

Everything legal in one page, still clear and still readable.

This page explains what WordGuesser may collect, what stays on your device, your license to use the app, and how to contact us if you need help.

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

WordGuesser is a party game app, and we collect and store a broad range of information about how you use the app and website. This can include account-related details, names or usernames, email addresses, gameplay data, selected game modes, selected languages, settings, custom content, and support communications.

We do not sell your personal data.

2. Information You Provide

Depending on the features you use, WordGuesser may process information you actively submit, including:

  • Your name, username, player name, or similar identifiers you enter.
  • Your email address when you contact us, create an account, or use account-linked features.
  • Your support messages, contact form submissions, screenshots, and email communications.
  • Custom word lists, prompts, or other content you create inside the app.
  • Basic account-related information if account or subscription features are enabled in your version of the app.

3. Information Collected Automatically

To operate, analyze, and improve the app, WordGuesser may collect extensive usage and technical data such as:

  • Anonymous or pseudonymous installation and session identifiers.
  • Gameplay analytics such as which games you play, selected categories, rounds played, player counts, and session duration.
  • Language, localization, and settings information such as selected language, app preferences, sound, haptics, and timers.
  • App usage, navigation, feature interaction, and similar behavioral analytics.
  • Basic technical information such as app version, device state, platform details, and error or diagnostic information.

4. Information Stored On Your Device

WordGuesser may store data locally on your device so the app feels fast and remembers your setup.

  • Onboarding choices and app preferences.
  • Game settings such as players, teams, timers, categories, sound, and haptics.
  • Custom words or prompts you add for your own rounds.

5. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Run the app, website, accounts, subscriptions, and related services.
  • Save your preferences, settings, language choices, and custom content.
  • Understand which game modes, languages, and features are being used.
  • Track usage patterns, improve gameplay, and guide future product decisions.
  • Fix bugs, monitor stability, troubleshoot problems, and improve performance.
  • Respond to support, privacy, account, and billing-related requests.

6. Third-Party Services

WordGuesser relies on third-party platforms and service providers. These may include Supabase for backend, storage, and analytics-related processing, RevenueCat for subscriptions and purchase-related services, Apple for App Store, billing, and platform functions, Expo and related SDK services used to build or operate the app, and Web3Forms for website contact form submissions. These providers may process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

We may retain collected data until it is deleted, no longer needed for operational purposes, or removed in response to a valid deletion request. Local data stored on your device may remain there until you delete the app or clear the relevant data.

8. Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can delete the app to remove locally stored data on your device.
  • You can contact us for privacy, support, or deletion requests.
  • If account features are available in your build, you can contact us to request account or stored data deletion assistance.

9. Children's Privacy

WordGuesser is a social party game and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate supervision or legal basis.

10. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at wordguesserinfo@gmail.com or use the Contact Us page.
